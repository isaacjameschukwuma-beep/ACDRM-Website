Overview

Governance Reform at Continental Scale

ARDGI is ACDRM's flagship multi-country governance programme, a five-year, donor-funded initiative designed to strengthen the institutions, policies, and coordination systems that underpin disaster risk governance across Africa.

Delivered across Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda, and Senegal, the programme integrates governance support, leadership development, disaster risk intelligence, and climate resilience into a single, coherent reform agenda.

Programme Components

Four Integrated Workstreams

Governance Support

Strengthening policy frameworks, legal structures, and inter-agency coordination for disaster risk management.

Leadership Development

Building the professional and executive leadership capacity that durable governance reform requires.

Disaster Risk Observatory

Embedding risk data, analytics, and hazard intelligence into national decision-making.

Climate Resilience

Integrating climate risk reduction into development planning and community resilience programmes.
