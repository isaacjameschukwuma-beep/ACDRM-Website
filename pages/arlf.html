Overview

Leadership for Africa's Resilience

The African Resilience Leadership Fellowship is designed for mid-career disaster management, humanitarian, and government professionals ready to step into senior leadership. Over six months of hybrid learning, Fellows build the strategic, technical, and ethical capabilities needed to lead institutions, not just respond to crises.

The Fellowship combines online learning, a residential academy, field visits, and expert mentorship, anchored in African operational realities and benchmarked against international professional standards.
