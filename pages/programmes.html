Programmes & Services

What We Deliver

Four core service lines, three flagship programmes, and specialist practice areas, each designed for the needs of governments, donors, and institutions across Africa.

Core Services

Four Service Lines

Advisory & Policy

  • Disaster risk governance assessments and institutional diagnostics
  • National and sub-national disaster risk policy design
  • Humanitarian coordination framework development
  • Strategic planning for emergency management agencies
  • Post-disaster needs assessments and recovery planning

Research & Knowledge

  • Applied disaster risk and climate resilience research
  • Policy briefs, toolkits, and institutional publications
  • Disaster risk data collection, analysis, and reporting
  • Continental and country-level risk profiling
  • Knowledge management system design

Training & Capacity Building

  • Certified professional training for emergency managers
  • Executive leadership development for government officials
  • Fellowship and academic programme delivery
  • Curriculum design and training manual development
  • Institutional training needs assessment

Resilience Financing

  • Disaster risk financing strategy design
  • Donor programme development and proposal preparation
  • Risk financing mechanism design for governments
  • Partnership structuring for multilateral engagements
  • Grant management advisory

Other Practice Areas

Specialist Technical Expertise

Beyond our six strategic pillars, ACDRM brings deep technical expertise across a set of high-demand specialised practice areas.

Humanitarian Coordination & Response

Design and strengthening of inter-agency coordination mechanisms, cluster systems, and humanitarian response architectures for complex emergencies.

Climate Risk & Adaptation Planning

Integrating climate risk assessment into national development planning, infrastructure design, and community resilience programmes.

Nuclear & Radiological Emergency Planning

Specialist advisory on nuclear and radiological hazard preparedness, response planning, and institutional readiness, benchmarked against international safety standards.

Business Continuity & Organisational Resilience

Supporting institutions to design and implement business continuity management systems that ensure operational resilience during and after disruption.

Early Warning Systems & Risk Technology

Advisory on the design, integration, and operationalisation of early warning systems, connecting data, technology, and community-level response protocols.

Post-Disaster Needs & Recovery

Post-disaster needs assessments and recovery planning that move institutions from immediate response to durable, systems-led recovery.
